Introduction:

BTS AKA Bangtan Sonyeondan(방탄소년단) is a South Korean boyband group that have 7 members. The group belongs to a record label called BigHit Entertainment owned by Bang Si-Hyuk who is a music producer and a former JYP composer. BTS debuted on June 13, 2013 with the song "No More Dreams". They have won several "New Artist of the Year" awards, including those at the 2013 MelOn Music Awards and Golden Disk Awards, and the 2014 Seoul Music Awards, as well as two major bonsang awards. The name of the band, Bangtan Sonyeondan, is a combination of "방탄" which means bulletproof and "소년단" which means boy scouts
