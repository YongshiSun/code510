What is Dragonboating?

  • Dragon Boating is a team racing sport that started in ancient China. With twenty paddlers, one drummer, and one steers-person on a boat; we work together to cross the finish line.
  • Although many people might not have heard of dragonboating, it is actually a really important part of Chinese history.
  • Many people today join in the dragonboating fun-- not only for fun and competition, but also to perpetuate the tradition of dragonboating.
  • In this sport, it's not just about strength, but also about timing, teamwork, and dedication.
  • There are many different ways to dragonboat, such as recreationally and competitively. You don't need to be competitive to have fun, but it doesn't hurt to win a medal or four. :)
